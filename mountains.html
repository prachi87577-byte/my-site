Majestic Mountains of India

Kanchenjunga (8586 m)

Location: Sikkim (India-Nepal border)

Significance: Third highest mountain in the world, sacred to Lepcha people

Trek Type: Restricted trekking (permits required)

Stay Options: Homestays in Yuksom, Hotels in Gangtok

Best Time: April to June, September to November

Nanda Devi (7816 m)

Location: Uttarakhand

Significance: Second highest peak in India, UNESCO biosphere reserve

Trek Type: Restricted zone (no summit access)

Stay Options: Joshimath hotels, Auli resorts

Best Time: May to October

Mount Abu (1220 m)

Location: Rajasthan

Significance: Only hill station in Rajasthan, spiritual and scenic

Trek Type: Easy hikes and sightseeing

Stay Options: Heritage hotels, Budget guesthouses

Best Time: October to March

Manirang (6593 m)

Location: Himachal Pradesh

Significance: Remote and rugged, ideal for experienced climbers

Trek Type: Expedition-style trek (guided only)

Stay Options: Camping, Local homestays

Best Time: July to September
